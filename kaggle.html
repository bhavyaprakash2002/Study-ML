Welcome to Kaggle!

Kaggle is the world's largest data science community! It is a platform where enthusiasts can compete, collaborate and learn. Whether you are a newbie or a professional or an expert, Kaggle has everything needed for you become extremely well in this skill.

I personally started kaggle after I had some elementry knowledge in the field. But, I would recommend you guys to start it when you are confident and good to go with some problems on your own.

I was asked some doubts regarding its functioning, competitions, resources, etc. I will try to address them all by explaining each component of Kaggle.

Home page

Your dashboard!

Starting with top-right sections you have your login-streak, tier progress and public activity. Tier progress tells you how much you have worked in the current tier to progress to next tier.

At the bottom you have your info about yourself i.e. how much notebooks you have created, your discussions/comments, datasets that you have created and posted on Kaggle, number of competitions that you have joined and lastly how many lessons/course you have completed.

Competitions

Kaggle Competitions is platform for competitive data science. Here, you have options for competing in all types of competitions whether they are related to biomed or real-life problems or anything. Competitions organized by companies also have prize pools for winners! Explore and challenge!

Datasets

Datasets are the collection of all the datasets that have been posted on Kaggle. Here you can upload your own datasets and explore trending ones so that you can practice on and off the competitions.

Models

Explore and use the ready-to-deploy models in your pipeline. These are the models which are developed by companies and are trained on Billions of parameters. You can use these models in your code to improve the performance.

Code

Your playground! Create you own notebooks for practicing or browse the trending notebooks on the platform.
